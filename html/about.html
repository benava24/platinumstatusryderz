About US

Platinum Status Ryderz (PSR), founded in October 2009, is an all-female motorcycle club driven by a passion for safe riding and community service. We are a group of mature, dedicated women who not only cherish the freedom of the open road but are also committed to promoting responsible, safe riding practices. Our mission is to create a culture of safety, support, and empowerment, ensuring that every member rides with confidence and responsibility. Beyond the road, we are deeply devoted to giving back to the community through volunteer work, charitable initiatives, and service projects that make a difference. At PSR, we believe in the power of sisterhood, where women come together to ride, protect one another, and serve the community with pride and purpose.
